Rome

Welcome to Rome, the capital of Italy, known for its nearly 3,000 years of globally influential art, architecture, and culture.

Key Attractions

  • Colosseum: An ancient amphitheater and a symbol of the Roman Empire.
  • Vatican City: The heart of the Catholic Church and home to the Pope.
  • Roman Forum: The center of public life in ancient Rome.
  • Trevi Fountain: A stunning Baroque fountain and a popular tourist attraction.
