Paris

Welcome to Paris, the prestigious tourist spot of France. Known as the "City of Light," Paris is renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture.

Key Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower: The iconic symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  • Louvre Museum: Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  • Champs-Élysées: A famous avenue known for its theaters, cafés, and luxury shops.
