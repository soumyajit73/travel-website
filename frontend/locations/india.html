India

Welcome to India, a land of diverse cultures, languages, and traditions. Known for its rich history and vibrant festivals.

Key Attractions

  • Taj Mahal: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of love.
  • Jaipur: Known as the "Pink City," famous for its palaces and forts.
  • Kerala Backwaters: A network of interconnected canals, rivers, and lakes.
  • Varanasi: One of the world's oldest inhabited cities, known for its ghats and temples.
