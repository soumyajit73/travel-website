Indonesia

Welcome to Indonesia, an archipelago of over 17,000 islands, known for its beaches, volcanoes, and diverse wildlife.

Key Attractions

  • Bali: Famous for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, and coral reefs.
  • Komodo National Park: Home to the Komodo dragon, the largest lizard on Earth.
  • Yogyakarta: Known for its traditional arts and cultural heritage.
  • Raja Ampat Islands: A paradise for divers with rich marine biodiversity.
